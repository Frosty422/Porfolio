About Me

Hi, I’m Cor — short for Hermias Cornelius Louw. I prefer Cor because it's easy to remember and, well, it just feels like me. I’m a freelance front-end web developer with a passion for crafting visual experiences that stick.
Image 2a
 While I currently work in web development, my long-term ambition reaches beyond the browser. I'm actively shifting toward game and cinematic design, particularly in character modeling and environment creation for cutscenes, cinematics, and CGI-based media. Whether it's a game, a short film, or a series like Love, Death & Robots, I'm driven by the chance to shape the visuals that captivate an audience.
What fuels me is the endless variety of styles, moods, and aesthetics people find compelling. I’m drawn to the challenge of merging these styles to create something uniquely alluring — something that not only looks good, but feels right. It’s more than design to me — it’s art.

 My strengths lie in learning quickly, adapting fast, and seeing visual details others might miss.
